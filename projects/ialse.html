About

IALSE is an organisation which discusses the life skills and related aspects with respect to the Indian society. It has various conferences all across India to spread awareness on the same issue and to bring up some solutions to contemporary problems. For their recent 2018 conference in Lucknow, we looked after the event print design needs.

Challenges

It was a huge event, with people from all across the country coming down to Lucknow to be a part of it. We were in charge of its full branding - from merchandise,invitations, and letterhead, to id cards, events brochure, and event backdrops. And presenting it in the right way was very important.

What we did

We carefully examined their previous works, to know then more closely. We found that the design has to be synchronous with the views of the organisation. And sinc a global audience, big speakers, and reputed media houses were associated with it, we placed 200% efforts.
