About

Bharat Rohan is a knowledge company empowering Indian farmers with extraordinary in-depth understanding of their lands and crop health. Dedicated to precision farming in India, BharatRohan is doing what India actually needs. Working with them for their Farmer’s Manual design, we learnt a lot from them.

Challenges

BharatRohan and its work are unique. Being exceptionally unconventional, they need to spread awareness and education about the same. We were supposed to create awareness, impart education and even attract farmers - through our design.

What we did

The Farmer’s Manual was a guide for the farmers to efficiently utilise the resources that BharatRohan is providing them. Keeping it simple, we incorporated the farmer’s interest in it, so that they use BharatRohan’s services. And ultimately, we took them a step ahead in revolutionizing the agriculture in India.
