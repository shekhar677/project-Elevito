About

R3 is a platform for the writers/artists to express their thoughts. Share them with the world - expressions that will connect with everyone, rhythms that will resonate with every heart. It presents writings in three beautiful linguistics - English, Hindi & Urdu, with the tag, with Love R3.

Challenges

Since a lot many online communities are running on social media, the challenge was to make it stand out. It had to connect with people, not just literature lovers, in least possible span of time.

What we did

We played with the layout and experimented a lot of colour schemes. To make a powerful brand line, which has its own unique social presence, we really spent a lot of time on social media. When the page likes grew magnanimously, we found that people were enjoying spending time with R3. And so we were!
