About

An engineering student aspired to write a romantic fiction novel and having done that, sought our help with the cover design. Mr. Irritating wanted his book cover to irritate the people and we kept this well in mind.

Challenges

Since the novel, Mr. Irritating was written in Hindi, we had to do the cover design in Hindi language. In order that the novel looks appealing, it must have a font which reflects the essence of Hindi. Yet, it should be modern. The challenge was getting the youngsters to connect with it.

What we did

It was for the very first time that we were working with Hindi fonts, but we came out with flying colours. The story, which had its three characters as the main highlight, was conveyed through the cover. As the novel got published, people fell in love with the cover, and the novel of course. That’s how we measure our success.
