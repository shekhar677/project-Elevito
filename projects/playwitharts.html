About

playwitharts is one of the emerging writing platforms in Hindi. As the name suggests, playwitharts aims at taking Hindi to all four corners of the world. It is working to bring all the artists, writers and readers under one roof, by providing them the platform (playwitharts) they need.

Challenges

While we were asked to do the brand identity for playwitharts, we had to come up with a design that reflects its aims and goals. We, as a design company had a great responsibility of promoting Hindi in this modern era through our design.

What we did

We designed its logo and manual with the sophisticated colour scheme. It was chosen after several changes so that it compliments the simple and decent font size. Once this was done, Hindi giri was all set to celebrate Hindi!
