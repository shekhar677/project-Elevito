About

TBT App was one of the platforms under the umbrella of The BBD Times, the official journalistic society of BBD group of Educational Institutions. It was an innovative initiative by the team to connect to each and every student. They wanted to utilise the digital medium and we did the native app design for them.

Challenges

As clearly visible, the challenge was to produce the best UI/UX for the mobile application. It had to be engaging and reflective of the aims and visions of the journalistic society.

What we did

To solve the design complexity and to bring up the coherence in the design, we had to understand the target audience. We had design a mobile app that connects with them, so that they connect with the management, college, students and its well being. The user is the ultimate boss and user experience speaks volume of your work. So we did it that way!
