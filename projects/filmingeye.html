About

Filming Eye is a film making startup, which won various film festivals and is trying to achieve more by connecting to the masses. It has a great command over connecting with the emotions, bringing stories to life everyday. We had to make the world see through their eyes and again, by our design. Also, they are one of our partners for year 2018.

Challenges

They wanted us to imagine more and create less. They wanted the design to arouse emotions in people and they themselves challenged us for the same. This was one of a kind challenge which increased the zeal we had for this project. And this is not a story. Their branding was thrown to us as a challenge.

What we did

We had become quite proficient in handling challenges by now and so, we imagined whatever story they told us. We imagined the world seeing through their eyes. And in a few hours, we did the designing. We lost the challenge, but they paid us for the work we did! Yeah, that’s what we did.
