About

The BBD Times, journalistic society of BBD Group of Educational Institutions, is an awesome and dedicated team, with their branding done by us. They brought innovation in the way college news was conveyed to the students and also organised grand events. They made every possible attempt to maximise their reach.

Challenges

The aim of this journalistic society was to solve student-related problems and initiate changes in the campus. We had a responsibility of designing their logo, which had to go on their mobile phone app and blog, along with their business stationery (letterhead, ID cards, brochures, etc.) and we couldnâ€™t make a mess. We had to keep it decent and inspiring.

What we did

We knew their audience comprised of majority of youth. We mixed the youthfulness with simplicity to bring out the decent and inspiring look from them. It went hand in hand with their tag line - Connect. Express. Grow.
